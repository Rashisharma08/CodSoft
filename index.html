Nikola Tesla

The genius who lit the world

Biography

Nikola Tesla was a Serbian-American inventor, electrical engineer, mechanical engineer, and futurist who is best known for his contributions to the design of the modern alternating current (AC) electricity supply system.

Born: July 10, 1856, Smiljan, Croatia

Died: January 7, 1943, New York City, USA

Achievements

  • Developed the AC induction motor and transformer
  • Invented the Tesla coil
  • Pioneered wireless communication and radio waves
  • Contributed to the development of X-ray technology

Famous Quote

"The present is theirs; the future, for which I really worked, is mine."
